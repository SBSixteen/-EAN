# Privacy Policy for EAN Studios

**Last Updated: July 20, 2026**

## 1. Introduction

EAN Studios ("we," "us," "our," or "the Company") develops and publishes mobile applications, including games and utility apps, available on the Google Play Store (collectively, the "Apps"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard information when you use our Apps.

By downloading, installing, or using any App published by EAN Studios, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with the terms of this policy, please do not use our Apps.

This policy applies to all Apps published by EAN Studios on Google Play. Individual Apps may have supplemental in-app disclosures, but this document governs unless a specific App states otherwise.

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Information Collected Automatically

When you use our Apps, certain information may be collected automatically through the App itself, through Google Play services, and through our advertising partner (Google AdMob):

- **Device Information**: device model, operating system and version, unique device identifiers (such as Android Advertising ID), language settings, system time and screen resolution.
- **Usage Data**: how you interact with the App, features used, session duration, in-app navigation, crash logs, and diagnostic data.
- **Approximate Location Data**: general location derived from IP address or device settings, primarily used for advertising relevance and analytics. We do not collect precise GPS location unless a specific App explicitly requests and discloses that permission for a core feature.
- **Advertising Identifiers**: Google Advertising ID (AAID) and similar identifiers used by AdMob to serve and measure ads.

### 2.2 Information You Provide Directly

Depending on the App, you may voluntarily provide:

- Support or feedback correspondence (e.g., emails sent to us).
- In-app settings or preferences you configure.
- Any content you choose to input into utility apps (e.g., notes, text, or files created within the app, which are typically stored locally on your device unless otherwise stated).

We do not require account creation with personal identifiers (such as name or email) for most Apps unless a specific App explicitly implements this feature, in which case that App's in-app disclosure will describe what is collected.

### 2.3 Local Save Data

Our games and utility apps store gameplay progress, scores, settings, and similar app data ("Save Data") **locally on your device only**, using standard Android local storage mechanisms. This Save Data is used solely to let you resume your progress or retain your preferences the next time you open the App.

- Save Data is **not transmitted to us, to Google AdMob, or to any third party**, and we do not have cloud servers that receive, store, or process it.
- We currently do **not** offer cloud save or cross-device sync for any App. If this changes in the future, we will update this Privacy Policy and the affected App's disclosures before enabling that feature.
- Save Data persists on your device until you clear the App's data/cache through Android system settings or uninstall the App, at which point it is permanently deleted from your device.
- Because Save Data is device-local, we have no ability to access, recover, or transfer it on your behalf (e.g., if you switch phones without cloud backup, that progress cannot be restored by us).

### 2.4 Purchase Information

Our Apps offer **in-app purchases** (one-time purchases; we do not offer subscriptions). All purchase transactions are processed by **Google Play's billing system**. We do not collect or store your payment card details, billing address, or full payment credentials — these are handled entirely by Google Play in accordance with Google's own privacy and payment policies. We may receive limited, non-financial transaction data (such as confirmation that a purchase was completed and which product was purchased) to unlock content within the App.

### 2.5 Information from Third-Party Services

Our Apps use **Google AdMob** to display advertisements. AdMob may collect and process data as described in Section 4 below. We do not control what data AdMob collects beyond what is configured through our AdMob account settings, and AdMob's data practices are governed by Google's own privacy policy.

## 3. How We Use Information

We use the information described above for the following purposes:

- **App Functionality**: to operate, maintain, and provide the core features of our games and utility apps.
- **Advertising**: to display ads through Google AdMob, including (where permitted by your consent and applicable law) personalized/interest-based ads, and to measure ad performance.
- **Analytics and Improvement**: to understand how our Apps are used, diagnose technical issues, fix bugs, and improve app performance and design.
- **Purchase Fulfillment**: to deliver in-app purchase content you have paid for, via Google Play's billing system.
- **Communication**: to respond to support requests, feedback, or inquiries you send us.
- **Legal Compliance**: to comply with applicable laws, regulations, legal processes, or enforceable governmental requests, and to enforce our terms and protect our rights.

We do **not** sell your personal information to third parties.

## 4. Advertising and Google AdMob

Our Apps are ad-supported through **Google AdMob**, a service provided by Google. AdMob may use device identifiers, approximate location, and app usage data to serve ads, including personalized ads based on your interests, and to measure ad effectiveness and prevent fraud.

Key points about our use of AdMob:

- AdMob and its partner networks may collect data as described in [Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy) and [How Google uses information from sites or apps that use its services](https://policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites).
- Where required by applicable law (such as in the European Economic Area, UK, or other jurisdictions with consent requirements), we present a **consent management platform (CMP)** allowing you to accept or reject personalized advertising and choose your ad preferences before ads are served.
- You may opt out of personalized advertising through your device settings:
  - **Android**: Settings > Google > Ads > "Opt out of Ads Personalization," or reset/delete your Advertising ID.
- Our Apps do **not** contain gambling features, real-money wagering, or simulated gambling mechanics of any kind, and we do not knowingly permit ads promoting gambling services to be shown to users where such ads would be inappropriate or restricted by Google Play policy.

## 5. Children's Privacy

We take children's privacy seriously. Some of our Apps may be designed for or accessible to a general audience that could include children.

- We do not knowingly collect personal information from children in a manner inconsistent with the **Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA)**, the **Google Play Families Policy**, or equivalent regulations (such as GDPR-K in the EU) in other jurisdictions.
- For any App directed at children or mixed audiences, we configure AdMob to serve only ads appropriate for that audience and restrict personalized advertising and data collection in line with Google Play's Families Policy requirements.
- If we become aware that we have inadvertently collected personal information from a child in violation of applicable law, we will take steps to delete that information promptly. Parents or guardians who believe their child has provided personal information to us may contact us using the details in Section 10.

## 6. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your personal data. We may share information in the following limited circumstances:

- **Service Providers**: with third parties that perform services on our behalf, such as Google (for Play Store distribution, billing, and AdMob advertising), analytics providers, and crash-reporting tools.
- **Legal Requirements**: if required to do so by law, regulation, legal process, or governmental request, or to protect the rights, property, or safety of EAN Studios, our users, or others.
- **Business Transfers**: in connection with a merger, acquisition, reorganization, or sale of assets, in which case information may be transferred as part of that transaction, subject to standard confidentiality protections.
- **With Your Consent**: for any other purpose disclosed to you at the time information is collected, with your consent.

## 7. Data Retention

We retain automatically collected usage and diagnostic data only as long as necessary for the purposes described in this policy (such as improving app performance) or as required by law. Advertising identifiers and related data are subject to Google AdMob's own retention practices. Save Data and any other content created within an App is stored locally on your device only, as described in Section 2.3, and is retained until you clear the App's data or uninstall the App.

## 8. Data Security

We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures designed to protect information from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. However, no method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

## 9. Your Rights and Choices

Depending on your jurisdiction (e.g., EEA/UK under GDPR, California under CCPA/CPRA, or other applicable privacy laws), you may have rights including the right to:

- Access the personal information we hold about you.
- Request correction or deletion of your personal information.
- Object to or restrict certain processing, including personalized advertising.
- Withdraw consent at any time (where processing is based on consent), without affecting the lawfulness of processing before withdrawal.
- Data portability, where applicable.
- Lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority.

Since most data we hold is processed via Google Play and AdMob rather than stored directly by us, many of these rights can also be exercised through your **Google Account privacy settings** and **Android device settings**. To exercise rights regarding data we control directly, contact us using the information in Section 10.
